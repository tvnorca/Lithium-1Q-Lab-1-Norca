Vhong Navarro

" height="300" alt="An image of Vhong Navarro" title="Vhong Navarro">

Vhong Navarro, whose real name is Ferdinand Hipolito Navarro, is a Filipino comedian, actor, dancer, recording artist, and television host. He is known for his comedic roles in films like Agent X44, Mr. Suave, and the Spider-Man parody Gagamboy. He is also a regular host on the ABS-CBN noontime variety show It's Showtime. Additionally, he was a member of the dance group Streetboys.
